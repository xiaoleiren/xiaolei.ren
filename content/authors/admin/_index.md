## About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, [Macau University of Science and Technology (MUST)](https://fie.must.edu.mo/id-1444/person/view/id-12435.html). I received my Ph.D. and M.S. from the University of Texas at Arlington, and my B.S. from Jilin University.

My research focuses on **software security** and **intelligent program analysis** — building automated tools that expose and eliminate vulnerabilities in complex software systems. This spans binary code analysis, compiler-level security, trustworthy AI, and embodied robotic systems.

I am actively looking for motivated students at all levels (undergraduate, master's, doctoral) to join my group. If you are passionate about security, systems, or AI-driven software engineering, I would love to hear from you — [reach out by email](mailto:xlren@must.edu.mo).
